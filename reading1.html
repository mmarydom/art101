Part A.

Make your own Drawing Language, Some Drawing Recipes and Art
Group assigned: Mary, Gio, and Mandy

Materials: 8 x 11 printer paper, Color Pencils, or pens

Units:
1. Pinky finger
2. a bunch (within a pinky finger (horizontally and vertically) radius)

Navigation:
1. Go horizontally Left
2. Go horizontally Right
3. Move vertically Up
4. Move vertically Down

Drawing Commands:
1. draw finger (from the tip of your finger to the end of your finger) Shapes = toppings:
2. Draw a circle= boba
3. Draw a rectangle = grass jelly
4. DrawD a Star= Fruit Color = flavor:
5. Fill area with orange= thai
6. Fill area with yellow= Mango
7. Fill area with brown= regular/black tea
8. Fill area with green = green tea

Repitition and Decision Control Structure:
1. Repeat step x on current position
2. Repeat step x and *navigatio n command*
3. Go back to position in step x
4. If lines intersect, move up, down, left, or right away from area

Interpretive Commands:
1. With the toppings, instead of drawing them individually, you use "bunch of'
2. You can use half a pinky for a shorter distance

Abstract and Summery:
We used our fingers as measure of units, as well as shapes that needed to be used. Unfortunately, the message wasn’t clear per person so each of us used our own shape. This error should have been established on the instructions so we would be using a constant shape. Although, this was good practice in realizing how important rules are when making and following them.

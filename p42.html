Project 4 (Wireframe and Mood board)

Proposal: For my final project, I want to do an interactive piece in which you explore a phone in order to find the owner. The story behind my concept is that you find someones phone in a random hallway and in order to find who it belongs to, you must explore the phone and its features. In a way, it is similar to that of a personal journal. The phone will have various apps are clickables and can be played with. Some apps I have in mind is a photo gallery, two game apps, notes, an app that requires finger print identification, a text msging app, and a few that are just there for aesthetic purposes.

The main focus or the main interactive component is the text messaging app, in which a random person texts you and you must respond back. You pick and choose the responses from 3 choices and the reciever should send a response back depending on the choice you choose. In any choice you choose, you find out the person responding back to you is the owner texting you through a friends phone.

Final Project

*PLEASE ZOOM OUT YOUR WEB BROWSER SCREEN TO 75% WHEN VIEWING FOR VISUALS TO ALIGN PROPERLY*
For this final project, you pose as a person who encounters a lost phone and must investigate who the owner is by explore the phone and its app. Each scene, or app, has small details that you must interpret in order to identify this phone's owner. Once finished, you can then answer the text messages an anonymous sender is sending you. I used theatrification as my interface context because my project imitates a phone and how it works, as well as the encorportation of a storyline and mission. The interative components do its job as imitating apps and text messaging. The system and behaviors aren't designed to be overly complex or complicated as the signs/symbols of the apps or buttons indicate the what the next scene will be. When interacted with, the scene simply changes to its specified destination. The design aesthetic imitates a cyberpunk theme or layout and color palette with realistic visuals that I edited and created through Adobe Photoshop.

A few technical features in this project:

- 1 help screen/main menu
- 30 states/screen = this includes the apps, homescreen, lockscreen, and the text messages and its individual choices
- 1 button (opens main menu) and 31 clickables
- 3 sounds (tap sound effects, main theme song, and the song played on the music app)
- 1 animated sprite in the google app
- 45 images made through Photoshop and 6 bitmap images floating

- libraries include:
- p5.play.js
- p5.sound.js
- p5.scenemanager.js
- p5.clickable.js

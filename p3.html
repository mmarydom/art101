Project 3

For this project, we were assigned to make our own clock using time funcitons. I decided to create a somewhat alice in wonderland theme where different images played the role of the time functions. Alice in my piece moves in a clockwise cycle in seconds. The Chesire cat's pupils moves in a counter clock-wise cycle and moves in minutes. Lastly, the white rabbit travels down horizontally by each hour. For the day and night cycle, I only have two different changes. In day, which is any time more than 12 hours, the color of the rectangle changes to white and the chesire is wide awake. At night, which is any time less than or equal to 12 hours, the rectangle pattern change to black and the chesire cat looks tired.
